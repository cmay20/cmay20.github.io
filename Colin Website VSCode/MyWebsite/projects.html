Flappy Face - Flappy Bird with Facial Recognition
×

AP Comp Sci Project | High School 2020


Link: (coming soon)


Idea: My parter and I needed to build a program for a class project. It took us a while to decide on an idea, but we knew we wanted to work with facial recognition and the OpenCV library. We ended up building a Flappy Bird game that uses your face as the controller. At first, We wanted to track the movement of your lips and fly the bird by opening and closing the mouth; however, we did not have enough time before the deadline. Because of the delayed start, we finished the project the day before the deadline. In the end, we received an excellent score from our teacher, had a lot of fun, and learned a great deal throughout the experience.



What We Used:


  • Python
  • OpenCV Library
